Our Services

We are a global custom decentralized application (Dapp) development company who focuses on solving problems and building products using blockchain technologies.

  • Decentralized Application (Dapp) development

    Decentralized applications are software application designed to take advantage of blockchain technologies so that they are maximally resilient to attack, error, and compromise.

  • Private blockchains

    Private blockchains provide the same benefits as a public blockchain, but can be customized to meet more specific needs, whether you are building an internal system for a large enterprise company or between numerous different partners.

  • Smart Contracts

    Smart Contracts are the future of a decentralized web. It allows for modular pieces of functionality to exist on its own and can be taken advantage of by anyone at anytime. These contracts are able to exchange digital tokens, which can represent any physical asset, good, or service. The possibilities are endless and what is possible is being discovered daily.

  • Consulting & Strategic discussions

    If you are looking for more information on blockchain technologies and smart contracts, we can help walk you through the pros and cons of this new computing paradigm. We can give you information resources and walkthroughs or do a deeper dive into the strategic implications of your current products.

  • Smart Contract auditing and review

    The development and design of smart contracts are still an active topic of research and discussion. Best Practices have not been fully formed yet, but some foundations are already being set. We can review any existing contracts of Dapps you have developed for security gaps, anti-patterns, or other areas of risk you should be aware of.
